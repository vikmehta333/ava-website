Frequently Asked Questions

Is cold email still effective in 2026?
Yes—when done right. Generic spam doesn't work, but personalized, value-driven outreach absolutely does. Our clients see 40%+ open rates and 15%+ reply rates because we focus on relevance and personalization, not volume.
How is this different from buying an email list?
We don't buy lists. We build targeted prospect lists through AI-powered research, verifying every contact and ensuring they match your ideal customer profile. This results in higher engagement and better deliverability.
Will my emails end up in spam?
We implement comprehensive deliverability best practices: domain warmup, SPF/DKIM authentication, reputation monitoring, and gradual volume ramping. Our average deliverability rate is 95%+.
How many emails do you send per day?
We typically start with 50-100 emails per day per domain to build reputation, then scale to 200-500+ as deliverability improves. Volume depends on your goals and the quality of your prospect list.
What kind of results can I expect?
Results vary by industry and offer, but our typical clients see: 35-45% open rates, 10-20% reply rates, and 2-5 qualified meetings booked per week. We focus on quality conversations, not just email volume.
